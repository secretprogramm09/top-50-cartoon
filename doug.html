Doug

Doug Meet Douglas Yancy Funnie, who just moved to Bluffington with his parents, Phil and Theda, and his sister, Judy. When he isn't playing with friends Skeeter, Patti and Beebe, Roger Klotz is one of Doug's rivals. Doug likes to write in his journal and fantasize that he is a superhero named Quailman. Program creators: Jim Jinkins, David Ray Campbell Writers: Alan J. Higgins Final episode date: June 26, 1999 Awards: Kids' Choice Award for Favorite Cartoon Networks: Nickelodeon, American Broadcasting Company

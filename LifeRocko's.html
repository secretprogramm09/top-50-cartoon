LifeRocko's Modern Life: Static Cling

LifeRocko's Modern Life: Static Cling Rocko's Modern Life: Static Cling is a 2019 American animated streaming television special based on, as well as a continuation of Joe Murray's Nickelodeon series Rocko's Modern Life. Initial release: August 9, 2019 Show: Rocko's Modern Life Directors: Joe Murray, Cosmo Segurson Nominations: GLAAD Media Award for Outstanding Kids & Family Programming Music composed by: Pat Irwin Distributed by: Netflix

The Powerpuff Girls

Accidentally created in a lab by Professor Utonium, superpowered sisters Blossom, Bubbles and Buttercup regularly save Townsville from evildoers such as Mojo Jojo, the Gangreen Gang and Him. Blossom is the self-proclaimed leader of the trio, Bubbles is the cute one and Buttercup is the toughest one. Awards: Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award for Outstanding Individual Achievement in Animation Predecessor: What a Cartoon! Program creator: Craig McCracken Composers: Thomas Chase; Steve Rucker; James L. Venable Ending theme: "The Powerpuff Girls (end theme)" (performed by Bis)

Looney Tunes

Looney Tunes is an American animated comedy short film series produced by Warner Bros. from 1930 to 1969, concurrently with its partner series Merrie Melodies, during the golden age of American animation. Directors: Chuck Jones, Tex Avery, Hugh Harman, Bob Clampett, Robert McKimson, Comics: Comic books Created by: Leon Schlesinger; Hugh Harman; Rudolf Ising Original work: Sinkin' in the Bathtub (1930) Owner: Warner Bros. (Warner Bros. Discovery)

Curious George

Curious George is a sweet African monkey who can't help but run into trouble. George's friend, "The Man in the Yellow Hat," tries very hard to care for George and is always saving the day. The show's themes are about learning, forgiveness and playful curiosity. Awards: Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Children's Animated Program Networks: PBS KIDS, PBS, Peacock Adapted from: Curious George Composer: Nick Nolan

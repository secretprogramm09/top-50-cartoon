Bugs Bunny

Bugs Bunny is an animated cartoon character created in the late 1930s at Warner Bros. Cartoons and voiced originally by Mel Blanc. Bugs is best known for his featured roles in the Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies series of animated short films, produced by Warner Bros. Creators: Chuck Jones, Tex Avery, Bob Clampett, Robert McKimson, Bob Givens Relatives: Clyde Bunny (nephew)
